Goa is the smallest state in India and is located in the southwestern region of the country. The mighty Arabian Sea forms the western coast of Goa. There are several places to visit in Goa for travelers with diverse interests, including nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, heritage explorers, and spiritual seekers. Being a tropical paradise, the state lures tourists with its splendid beaches and exotic resorts. Tourists can enjoy a variety of activities, such as swimming, sunbathing, fishing, windsurfing, waterskiing, and scuba diving. Goa is also rich in natural beauty with its lush green forests and rivers. The state is home to some of the best wildlife sanctuaries and national parks in India where tourists can spot a variety of rare and endangered animals.
